NorthShore Executive Counseling focuses on helping high-performing professionals navigate burnout, sustained stress, and the impact these have on both performance and relationships.

This work is grounded in a practical understanding of high-responsibility environments. Before transitioning to counseling, Joseph spent over twenty years working in finance and accounting, including experience as a Certified Public Accountant. In addition to a Master of Arts in Counseling from Northwestern University, he also holds an MBA.

This background informs a direct, structured approach that aligns closely with how professionals think, operate, and make decisions. The focus is not only on reducing stress, but on restoring clarity, consistency, and sustainable performance.

Clients often seek this work when they want to feel sharper, more in control, and more present—both in their work and in their relationships at home.
