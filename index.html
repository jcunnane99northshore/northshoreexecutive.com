Approach

Executive wellness is a systems issue, not a willpower issue.

Burnout rarely begins with a collapse. It tends to show up as decision overload, constant activation, reduced patience, and a subtle loss of sharpness. My work focuses on identifying the patterns driving that strain and replacing them with a way of operating that is sustainable.

Structured

This is focused work with a clear direction. We identify what is driving burnout and target the factors that are keeping it in place.

Practical

The work is grounded in actionable changes that affect how you think, recover, perform, and relate under pressure.

Discreet

The practice is designed for professionals who value privacy, directness, and a service that respects the realities of a demanding role.

Who It’s For

A strong fit for professionals whose success has started to feel expensive.

From the outside, things may still look strong. Internally, your focus may be slipping, your patience may be shorter, or it may feel harder to fully disconnect from work. That is often the point where burnout starts to compound.

You may notice:

  • Decision-making takes longer than it used to
  • Mental fatigue shows up earlier in the day
  • Work is harder to shut off, even at home
  • Relationships are feeling the impact of stress
  • You are still functioning, but not at your best

The work focuses on:

  • Restoring clarity and cognitive bandwidth
  • Reducing emotional reactivity under pressure
  • Interrupting burnout before it deepens
  • Rebuilding sustainable routines that actually hold
  • Maintaining performance without constant depletion

About

Direct, thoughtful, and built for people carrying a lot.

I am a Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor in Illinois. My approach integrates evidence-based work with practical structure, helping clients understand what is driving burnout while making meaningful changes in how they function day to day.

What informs the work

The practice draws from cognitive behavioral therapy, acceptance and commitment therapy, and psychodynamic insight where useful. The emphasis is not just on insight. It is on helping you operate with greater steadiness, intention, and resilience under sustained demand.
